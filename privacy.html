Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: April 2026

Straightforward Privacy: We only keep your data long enough to do the job you hired us for — then we securely delete it after 7 days. No exceptions.

1. The 7-Day Deletion Rule (Our Core Promise)

We use encrypted email services to receive your documents. Exactly 7 days after we deliver your audit, your resume, target job postings, and email thread are permanently deleted from our systems.

2. What We Collect and Why

  • Email Address: To communicate with you and deliver your audit.
  • Resume & Job Postings: To perform the ATS compliance audit.
  • Payment Data: Handled entirely by our payment processors (Stripe or your BTC wallet). We never see, touch, or store your credit card information.

3. What We Will NEVER Do

  • We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information.
  • We will never use your resume to train public AI models. Any AI tools used internally during our HIL (Human-in-Loop) process are configured for zero-data-retention.
  • We will never spam you with marketing emails.

4. 7-Day Refund Policy

We stand by our work. If our audit does not meet your expectation, simply email for a full refund: audit@stoicresume.com within 7 days of receiving your report, and we will process the refund. No questions asked.

5. GDPR & Your Rights

If you are accessing this site from the EU or UK, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or immediate deletion of your personal data before the standard 7-day auto-deletion window. Email us to enforce these rights.
