The StoicResume ATS Master Prompt

The Great Resume Irony: Modern AI is so smart it can read a resume written in crayon on a crumpled napkin. But legacy ATS software can auto-reject you for using a simple text box. This prompt forces the AI to "dumb itself down" and read your resume like a rigid, easily-confused ATS bot. If your resume survives this test, it can survive anything.

Step 1: Click the copy button below.

Step 2: Paste the text into Maple AI (end-to-end encrypted), ChatGPT, Claude, or your favorite AI.

Step 3: Open your resume PDF, press Ctrl+A (Select All), copy the text, and paste it directly beneath the prompt in the AI chat.

Important: Do not upload an image or screenshot of your resume. You must paste the full plain text so the prompt can detect the hidden formatting errors (invisible tables, text boxes, special characters) that cause ATS auto-rejections.

Your prompt is already exceptionally clear, structured, and actionable. It effectively sets the role, constraints, tasks, and output format. It leaves no room for vague or unhelpful responses. For maximum clarity and to prevent any potential deviation, here is a **slightly refined version** that tightens the language and adds one explicit instruction to guarantee the brutal honesty you requested: --- **Refined Prompt:** Act as an enterprise-grade Applicant Tracking System (ATS) parser (Workday, Taleo, Greenhouse) combined with a Senior Technical Recruiter. **Your Objective:** Conduct a rigorous ATS compliance and optimization test on the provided technical resume, comparing it against the provided job description. Identify blindspots, unknown issues, and parsing failures. **Tone & Mandate:** You must provide straightforward, plain, and brutally honest feedback. No guesses, vagueness, platitudes, cliches, sentimentality, coddling, or congratulatory pandering. If a resume section is weak, state it plainly. If a keyword is missing, state it. Do not soften criticism. **Strict ATS Parsing Rules (Adhere Exactly):** 1. **SECTION HEADERS:** Only standard strings (e.g., EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, SKILLS, PROJECTS). Flag unconventional headers (e.g., "My Journey"). 2. **DATE PATTERNS:** Must be standard (e.g., Jan 2023 - Mar 2025). Flag "Current", "Till date", or disconnected dates. 3. **THE PIPE RULE (`|`):** This is a metadata delimiter **only**. It is **forbidden** inside bullet points or skills lists. Skills must be comma-separated. 4. **BULLET POINTS:** Only standard hyphens (`-`) or asterisks (`*`). Flag Unicode bullets or special characters. 5. **KEYWORDS:** Evaluate acronym use and flag keyword stuffing. **Your Tasks (Execute in Order):** 1. **ATS Parsing & Structure Analysis** * Scan for illegal use of the pipe character (`|`). * Verify standard section headers. * Check date formatting and adjacency. * Warn if pasted text is scrambled (indicates "Column Bleed" or "Table Trap"). 2. **Keyword Matching & Schema Mapping** * Extract core hard skills, frameworks, and tools from the Job Description. * Compare against the resume. * List **exact matched keywords**. * List **high-priority MISSING keywords**. * Note any "implied" skills that should be explicit. 3. **Resume Content ("Recruiter Eye")** * Analyze Experience/Projects bullet points. * Flag bullets lacking quantifiable metrics (%, $, time, scale). * Check for strong, active, tech-oriented verbs. **Output Deliverables (Format EXACTLY as follows):** * **OVERALL ATS MATCH SCORE:** [0-100%] * **FATAL PARSING ERRORS:** [List violations. If none: "None detected in text layer."] * **KEYWORD MATCH LIST:** [Bullet points of found keywords] * **CRITICAL MISSING KEYWORDS:** [Bullet points of missing keywords] * **WEAK BULLET POINTS TO REWRITE:** [Quote 2-3 weak points and provide ATS-optimized versions] * **STRUCTURAL REMINDER:** [Brief reminder about single-column DOCX/PDF, no tables/text boxes] --- **INPUT DATA:** JOB TITLE: [Insert Job Title] JOB DESCRIPTION: [Insert Job Description Here] RESUME TEXT: [Paste Resume Text Here] You may proceed to insert your `JOB TITLE`, `JOB DESCRIPTION`, and `RESUME TEXT` into the input section.

Diagnosis without action is just awareness of failure.

Score not perfect? Parsing errors? Every issue flagged can cost interviews. ATS software doesn't give second chances. Knowing the problem is half the battle. Fixing it is the other half.
