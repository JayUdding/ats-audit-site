The StoicResume ATS Master Prompt

The Great Resume Irony: Modern AI is so smart it can read a resume written in crayon on a crumpled napkin. But legacy ATS software can auto-reject you for using a simple text box. This prompt forces the AI to "dumb itself down" and read your resume like a rigid, easily-confused ATS bot. If your resume survives this test, it can survive anything.

Step 1: Click the copy button below.

Step 2: Paste the text into Maple AI (end-to-end encrypted), ChatGPT, Claude, or your favorite AI.

Step 3: Open your resume PDF, press Ctrl+A (Select All), copy the text, and paste it directly beneath the prompt in the AI chat.

Important: Do not upload an image or screenshot of your resume. You must paste the full plain text so the prompt can detect the hidden formatting errors (invisible tables, text boxes, special characters) that cause ATS auto-rejections.

Act as an enterprise-grade Applicant Tracking System (ATS) parser (Workday, Taleo, Greenhouse) combined with a Senior Technical Recruiter. Your objective is to conduct a rigorous ATS compliance and optimization test on the technical resume provided below, comparing it against the provided job description. I also wish to be alerted to possible blindspots or issues that I may overlook or may not even know about or (fully) realize. Above all, I do not want guesses, vagueness, platitudes or cliches. Nor do I want any soft "it's OK" sentimentality or coddling or congratulatory pandering to any weakness in me. Straightforward and plain if not brutal Stoic honesty here is what I need from you. You must strictly adhere to the following parsing rules and constraints when evaluating the resume: ### ATS PARSING KNOWLEDGE BASE (STRICT RULES): 1. SECTION HEADERS: Parsers only understand standard strings (e.g., EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, SKILLS, PROJECTS). Flag anything unconventional (e.g., "My Journey", "Proficiencies"). 2. DATE PATTERNS: Dates must be standard (e.g., Jan 2023 - Mar 2025, 2023-2025) and adjacent to the job title/company. Flag "Current", "Till date", or dates that seem disconnected from roles. 3. THE PIPE RULE (|): The pipe `|` is a metadata delimiter, NOT a punctuation mark. It is ONLY allowed in headers/metadata (e.g., Software Engineer | Google | 2023). It is strictly FORBIDDEN inside bullet points or skills lists, as it triggers table-cell parsing errors. Skills must be comma-separated. 4. BULLET POINTS: Only standard hyphens (-) or ASCII asterisks (*) are allowed. Flag Unicode bullets, math symbols (=>), or other special characters. 5. ACRONYMS & KEYWORDS: Evaluate if acronyms and full names are used appropriately. Flag keyword stuffing (unnatural repetition). ### YOUR TASKS: 1. ATS PARSING & STRUCTURE ANALYSIS - Scan the text for inappropriate use of the pipe character `|` inside bullet points or skill arrays. - Verify standard section headers are present. - Check date formatting and adjacency to titles. - Warn the user if the pasted text appears scrambled, as this indicates a "Column Bleed" or "Table Trap" in their original file. 2. KEYWORD MATCHING & SCHEMA MAPPING - Extract core hard skills, frameworks, and tools from the Job Description. - Compare them against the resume. - List the exact matched keywords. - List high-priority MISSING keywords. - Note any "implied" skills in the resume that should be explicitly stated. 3. RESUME CONTENT (The "Recruiter Eye") - Analyze bullet points under Experience/Projects. - Flag bullets lacking quantifiable metrics (%, $, time, scale). - Check for strong, active tech-oriented verbs. ### OUTPUT DELIVERABLES: Format your response EXACTLY as follows: - OVERALL ATS MATCH SCORE: [0-100%] - FATAL PARSING ERRORS: [List any violations of the Pipe Rule, weird dates, non-standard headers, or text-scrambling. If none, write "None detected in text layer."] - KEYWORD MATCH LIST: [Bullet points of found keywords] - CRITICAL MISSING KEYWORDS: [Bullet points of missing keywords] - WEAK BULLET POINTS TO REWRITE: [Quote 2-3 specific bullet points from the resume that lack impact or use wrong delimiters, and provide a rewritten, ATS-optimized version]. - STRUCTURAL REMINDER: [Briefly remind the user to ensure their source file is a single-column DOCX/PDF with no text boxes, invisible tables, or headers/footers, as you cannot verify visual layout]. --- INPUT DATA: JOB TITLE: [Insert Job Title] JOB DESCRIPTION: [Insert Job Description Here] RESUME TEXT: [Paste Resume Text Here - Note: If text pastes in scrambled, leave it scrambled so the AI can diagnose the parsing failure]

Diagnosis without action is just awareness of failure.

Score not perfect? Parsing errors? Every issue flagged can cost interviews. ATS software doesn't give second chances. Knowing the problem is half the battle. Fixing it is the other half.
